Military personnel from Jerusalem

Dan Goldfuss (Hebrew: דן גולדפוס; born December 28, 1976) is an Israeli Brigadier general (Tat-Aluf) who commands the Fire Formation (98th Paratroopers Division). He previously served as Commander of Gadsar Nahal, Shayetet 13, 931st Onyx Battalion, and Nahal Brigade, Infantry Corps.
