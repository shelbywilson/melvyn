People with post-traumatic stress disorder

Complex post-traumatic stress disorder (CPTSD) is a stress-related mental disorder theoretically happening in response to complex traumas, i.e. generally prolonged or repetitive exposures to a series of traumatic events, within which individuals perceive few or no chance to escape.CPTSD differs from somatization disorder, dissociative identity disorder, and borderline personality disorder.
