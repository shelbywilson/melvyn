Love stories

1942: A Love Story, also known as 1942 A Love Story, is a 1994 Indian Hindi-language patriotic romance film, directed by Vidhu Vinod Chopra. It stars Anil Kapoor, Manisha Koirala and Jackie Shroff, with Anupam Kher, Danny Denzongpa, Pran and Brian Glover.
