Ancient Egyptian mummies

The ancient Egyptians had an elaborate set of funerary practices that they believed were necessary to ensure their immortality after death. These rituals included mummifying the body, casting magic spells, and burials with specific grave goods thought to be needed in the afterlife.The ancient burial process evolved over time as old customs were discarded and new ones adopted, but several important elements of the process persisted.
