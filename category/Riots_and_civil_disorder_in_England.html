Riots and civil disorder in England

The 2011 England riots, more widely known as the London riots, were a series of riots between 6 and 11 August 2011. Thousands of people rioted in cities and towns across England, which saw looting, arson, as well as mass deployment of police and the deaths of five people.The protests started in Tottenham Hale, London, following the death of Mark Duggan, a local man who was shot dead by police on 4 August.
