Recipients of the Order of the Medjidie, 1st class

Order of the Medjidie (Ottoman Turkish: نشانِ مجیدیه, August 29, 1852 – 1922) was a military and civilian order of the Ottoman Empire. The order was instituted in 1851 by Sultan Abdulmejid I. == History == Instituted in 1851, the order was awarded in five classes, with the First Class being the highest.
