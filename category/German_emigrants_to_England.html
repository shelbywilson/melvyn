German emigrants to England

Lukas Okechukwu Nmecha (born 14 December 1998) is a German professional footballer who plays as a forward for Bundesliga club VfL Wolfsburg and the Germany national team. Nmecha was born in Hamburg, Germany but relocated to England as a child with his family.
