20th-century British non-fiction writers

William Andrew Murray Boyd (born 7 March 1952) is a Scottish novelist, short story writer and screenwriter. == Biography == Boyd was born in Accra, Gold Coast, (present-day Ghana), to Scottish parents, both from Fife, and has two younger sisters.
