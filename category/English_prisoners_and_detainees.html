English prisoners and detainees

Charles Arthur Salvador (formerly Charles Ali Ahmed; born Michael Gordon Peterson on 6 December 1952), better known as Charles Bronson, is a British criminal, a violent and notorious life prisoner. He has spent periods detained in the Rampton, Broadmoor and Ashworth high-security psychiatric hospitals.
