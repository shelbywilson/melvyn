Curator of Middle Eastern Coins at the British Museum

Episodes

  1. Persepolis↗

    Melvyn Bragg and guests discuss the 'City of the Persians' that flourished for almost 200 years from the reign of Darius I until its destruction by Alexander III of Macedon.

  2. Shahnameh of Ferdowsi↗

    Melvyn Bragg and his guests discuss the epic poem the Shahnameh of Ferdowsi, the 'Book of Kings', which has been at the heart of Persian culture for the past thousand years.

  3. The Sassanid Empire↗

    Melvyn Bragg and guests discuss the Sassanian Empire, a grand imperial rival to the Roman Empire.

  4. Zoroastrianism↗

    Melvyn Bragg discusses Zoroastrianism, often claimed to be the world's first monotheistic religion, and known to have passed on ideas of the afterlife to Judaism.
