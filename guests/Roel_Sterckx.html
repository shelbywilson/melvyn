Episodes

  1. Li Shizhen↗

    Melvyn Bragg and guests discuss the work of the Chinese doctor who investigated natural remedies, under the Ming, and compiled the greatest collection of treatments of his time

  2. Chinese Legalism↗

    Melvyn Bragg and guests discuss the rise of Chinese Legalism under the First Emperor, its relationship with Confucianism and its legacy today.

  3. Sources of Early Chinese History↗

    Melvyn Bragg and his guests discuss early Chinese history and the documents in which it was recorded.

  4. The Han Synthesis↗

    Melvyn Bragg discusses the Han Synthesis, an overarching system of thought which still defines Chinese culture today.
