Jill Kraye

Professor of the History of Renaissance Philosophy and Librarian at the Warburg Institute, University of London

Episodes

  1. Scepticism↗

    Melvyn Bragg and his guests discuss the history of philosophical scepticism.

  2. Erasmus↗

    Melvyn Bragg and his guests discuss the life and work of the Dutch humanist scholar Desiderius Erasmus, one of the most significant figures of the Renaissance.

  3. The School of Athens↗

    Melvyn Bragg and guests discuss the Italian Renaissance artist Raphael's depiction of Plato and Aristotle and what it tells us about both the subjects and the painter.
